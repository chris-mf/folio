A.H. Beard had a vision of bed that could track and adapt to your changing sleep needs over time.

Adpatable features

With Built in tracking, firmness adjustments, and adjustable frame meant that the bed could adapt to changing needs over time.

Inbuilt sleep sensors allowed users to understand the optimal firmness for their body.

Each side of the bed is individually controlled by the app, allowing users to optimise comfort.

Multiple pre-set and customisable positions were available to enable resting in the most comfortable way.

A unique design & technology challenge

Due to hardware constraints, one challenge that arose was pairing the mattress with the users smartphone. Through multiple rounds of testing we were able to design a flow that enabled users to successfully set up each part of the mattress without, audio, visual or kinesthetic prompts.

Working in collaboration with the mattress engineering team at the factory, we were able to uncover and solve for pain-points that would arise during interaction with the app, such as no serial numbers to reference devices for troubleshooting.
