Digital Driver's License

Human-centric approach towards government services.

As part of the ongoing push to digitize government services & create better experiences for their citizens, the NSW government is currently developing a digital drivers licence. Service NSW wanted to better understand how citizens would use the new licence, to inform both product & policy development.

Over 5 weeks a series of citizen co-design sessions were ran, combined with a rapid cadence of prototyping and testing. This gave invaluable insight into citizens concerns around data security, control of their information and accessibility.

Privacy first approach

Due to hardware constraints, one challenge that arose was pairing the mattress with the users smartphone. Through multiple rounds of testing we were able to design a flow that enabled users to successfully set up each part of the mattress without, audio, visual or kinesthetic prompts.
